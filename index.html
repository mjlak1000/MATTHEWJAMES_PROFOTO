Artist Statement

This collection explores the intersection of urban decay and human presence, capturing the raw beauty found in Portland's industrial landscapes and forgotten spaces.

Through high-contrast black and white imagery interspersed with moments of color, each photograph reveals the textures, patterns, and quiet poetry of the everyday urban experience.

The work emphasizes atmosphere and mood, inviting viewers to see familiar city scenes through a lens of contemplation and artistic transformation.

Mixed Media

Beyond the Lens

Beyond photography, my practice extends into mixed media and abstract art. These works explore the same themes — decay, transformation, layered histories — but through gestural mark-making, color theory, and digital manipulation.

Each piece builds through multiple passes: painting, drawing, collage, and digital layering, creating dense visual compositions that reward extended contemplation.
